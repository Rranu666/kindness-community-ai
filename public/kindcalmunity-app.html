What Is KindCalmUnity?

A Safe, Harmonious
Network for Families

A cooperative support system where neighbours take turns so everyone can contribute, and everyone gets a well-earned break. No family is an island. Together, the load is lighter, the joy is richer, the community is stronger.

🍲

👶

Childcare Circles

🥕

🚗

Carpool Networks

From park mornings to craft evenings — organised collectively and enjoyed by all.

Four simple steps to a thriving cooperative circle in your neighbourhood.

🏡

🔄

The Rotation Runs

Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining or starting a KindCalmUnity circle.

How many families do I need to start a circle?
+
You can start with as few as 3 families and grow from there. We recommend 6–10 for a well-balanced rotation. Our circle wizard helps you set expectations and agreements from the very start.
How does the fair rotation work?
+
The KindCalmUnity rotation system cycles every family through each service they have opted into. The system tracks contributions to ensure no family carries a disproportionate load, and you can request turn swaps anytime.
Is KindCalmUnity safe for childcare sharing?
+
Safety is our highest priority. All childcare circle members are verified by the circle administrator, and each circle sets its own safety agreements — background checks, house rules, emergency contacts, and child preferences. We provide templates and guidance for every step.
What if I cannot fulfil my turn due to illness or life events?
+
Life happens — and KindCalmUnity is designed for it. You can request a turn swap with any other circle member at any time. Circles are encouraged to build in a "compassion buffer" for unexpected life events.
Does it cost anything to join or use KindCalmUnity?
+
KindCalmUnity is a Kindness Community Foundation initiative — our tools and coordination platform are free for community use. We believe the infrastructure of kindness should be accessible to all families, regardless of income.
Can we choose only some services, like just meals or just carpools?
+
Absolutely. Circles are fully customisable. Many circles start with just one service (often meals or carpools) and gradually expand as trust and confidence grows. Individual families can opt into only the services they are comfortable with.
