About Kindness Community Foundation

KCF is dedicated to building structured, transparent, and technology-assisted infrastructure that enables communities to coordinate volunteer efforts, contributions, and mutual aid at scale. Founded in Newport Beach, California, we serve communities across the United States.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is a world where every community has access to sustainable, ethical infrastructure for collective kindness and mutual aid. Our mission is to promote community stabilization through transparent governance, ethical technology, and structured volunteer coordination.

Our Key Initiatives

  • KindWave — A real-time community kindness mapping platform connecting people who need help with those who can offer it.
  • ServeKindness — Structured volunteer coordination for meaningful, trackable community service.
  • Community Giving — Transparent donation infrastructure ensuring every contribution reaches its intended community purpose.
  • KindWave App — Mobile technology bringing community support networks to your fingertips.

Leadership & Governance

KCF operates under transparent governance with a volunteer board of directors committed to ethical leadership, community accountability, and sustainable nonprofit practices.
